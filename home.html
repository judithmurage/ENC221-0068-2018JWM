Namanga Forest Project

Welcome to the Namanga Forest Project website. We are dedicated to the conservation and preservation of the beautiful Namanga Forest.

The Namanga Forest is a lush and biodiverse forest located in the border region between Kenya and Tanzania. It plays a vital role in supporting local ecosystems and providing habitat for various plant and animal species.

Our project aims to raise awareness about the importance of preserving this unique forest ecosystem. We conduct research, organize community initiatives, and work with local stakeholders to ensure the sustainable management of the Namanga Forest.
