About Me

Hello! I'm passionate about exploring and promoting the natural beauty of the Namanga Forest.

One key aspect of the Namanga Forest is its extensive coverage, encompassing a significant area of land. In terms of percentage, the Namanga Forest covers approximately XX% of the border region between Kenya and Tanzania.

As for tourist attractions, the Namanga Forest boasts a diverse array of flora and fauna. Visitors can witness the enchanting beauty of towering trees, discover rare plant species, and encounter a variety of wildlife, including birds, mammals, and insects.

To get to the Namanga Forest, you can follow these directions:

  1. From Nairobi, head south on the A104 highway towards Namanga town.
  2. Upon reaching Namanga, follow the signs towards the forest entrance.
  3. There are designated walking trails within the forest for visitors to explore its wonders.

Join me in experiencing the remarkable natural wonders of the Namanga Forest!
